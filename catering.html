Catering

If you would like us to provide the food for your gathering or event, please call the restaurant phone (do not email!) Note that we do not deliver. Catering is only available for pre-order of two weeks in advance.

Please:

Look at the dinner menu as you make your choices (there is no separate catering menu) Come early with (or drop off ahead of time) the containers in which we will pack your food Note that around 3pm is the earliest that a large order (15-20ppl) can be picked up.
